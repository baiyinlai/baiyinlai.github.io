    About Me

    My name is Baige5117. I am an twenty-x years old graduate student majoring in Mechanical and Electronic Engineering. I was born, grew up and currently live in China, an ancient civilization with a long history and splendid culture where amazing happens everyday.

    I spend most of my time sleeping, surfing the Internet and doing research (mainly about CAD/CAM, specifically reverse engineering). I have a great diversity of interests, such as reading, listening to music and travelling. I love sports very much too, especially basketball. Actually, I love all things interesting, creative and challenging.

    About the Site

    This site originally came from the need to have a single place to keep my notes and writings, but now principally as a way to describe every detail of my ordinary life, to record what I see, hear and feel which touch my soul, and to log my study notes. Nothing special, just a garden for recording and recalling.

    I make this website with Jekyll engine completely DIY in my free time. The design was created from scratch based on the style and layout of my another blog. Everything was made on Emacs, nothing else.
